Packet Format:
All bytes are floats.

[bytes 1-4] impact - Peak acceleration magnitude around impact, range 5.0-15.0
[bytes 5-8] follow through angle - How much the club rotates after impact, range 40.0-80.0
[bytes 9-12] tempo - downswing/backswing, 0.4-0.6
[bytes 13-16] stability - How much the club is rotating at the exact moment of impact, 0-15 
[bytes 14 - 20] straightness - Acceleration perpendicular to swing axis, 0-3
[bytes 21 - 24] direction - right = 1, left = 0, ball not detected = 0.5
[bytes 25 - 28] result - miss = 0, hit = 1
